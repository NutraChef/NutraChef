About NutraChef

NutraChef is a professional online consultancy offering expert guidance in nutrition, food service, and wellness. We specialize in science-based dietary solutions for individuals, groups, and institutions.

Core Services

  • Diet and Nutrition Counselling (Individual or Group): Personalized nutrition guidance to help individuals or teams achieve health goals through sustainable and science-based dietary strategies.
  • Group Workshops and Webinars on Healthy Diet and Wellness: Interactive and evidence-based sessions on nutrition, lifestyle, and wellness topics for schools, companies, and community groups.
  • Meal and Menu Planning (Individual or Institutionalized): Custom meal plans and menu cycles tailored to specific dietary needs, preferences, or institutional guidelines (e.g., schools, hospitals, food businesses).
  • Recipe Development and Nutrition Labelling: Creation and standardization of recipes with nutrient analysis, costing, and nutrition facts labeling—ideal for product development or publication.
  • Food Costing and Profitability Tools: Smart tools and templates for accurate costing, pricing strategies, and inventory planning to boost food business profitability.
  • Food Branding and Marketing: Visual and strategic support for developing your food brand—menus, labels, social media content, and marketing collaterals with nutrition credibility.
